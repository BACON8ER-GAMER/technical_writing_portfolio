About Nate

Nate was born in Seattle, Washington, and has lived in the Pacific Northwest the majority of his life. Since high school, he developed a passion for writing during college and has since pursued a career in technical writing, taking classes in the University of Washington's Technical Writing certificate program. He has a passion for technology, having been able to work a TV remote at the age of two. He currently lives in Kenmore, Washington.
