About Nate

Nate was born in Seattle, Washington, and has lived in the Pacific Northwest the majority of his life. Since high school, he developed a passion for writing during college and has since pursued a career in technical writing, taking classes in the University of Washington's Technical Writing certificate program. He has a passion for technology, having been able to work a TV remote at the age of two.

Nate has a Bachelor of Arts in Music Production from Pacific University, and is currently enrolled in the Professional Technical Writing Certificate program at the University of Washington. He is expected to graduate in November 2025. He currently lives in Kenmore, Washington.
